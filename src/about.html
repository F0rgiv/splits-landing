About the Creators

James brings over 10 years of sys admin experience with a focus in cloud, virtualization and automation. He’s excited about technology and specifically the different ways that it enables creativity in people and enables them to share and create things otherwise impossible.

Michael brings 10 years of music industry experience managing indie artists, their catalogs and their royalties. He's excited about technology and how it can make the music world a better place.

After spending many an hour working on custom Excel files to help manage and track the ownership of songs and albums, (not to mention the payouts of any sales, streams etc. to artists, producers and labels), we started to ask ourselves, "is there a better way?" Turns out that unless you were willing to fork out some serious coin there wasn’t. Thus beginning our journey with Splits.


Together we are working to create a SaaS application that can function efficiently and cheaply to resolve royalty accounting issues for both large labels and small indie artist. All without the headaches of a custom solution that would end up costing more than your earnings.


As artists and managers ourselves we know firsthand the pains and challenges of growing sales in the industry and are working to resolve them for ourselves and you!

Planned features:

Song and album tracking including ownership percentages for artists and producers as well as publishing fees. Simple aggregation of sales data from multiple locations including Spotify, Youtube, Apple Music, Tunecore and many others. Automatic tracking and calculations of payouts that need to occur for each individual with ownership of a track. Automatic reports for everyone, showing where their earnings are coming from as well as more broad summaries for entire bands or labels. Automatic payment processing for individuals. A solid solution to world hunger.
