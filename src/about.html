After spending many an hour working on custom excel files to help manage and track the ownership of songs and albums. Not to mention the payouts of any sales, streams etc. to the artists, producers and labels. We started to ask ourselves. Is there was a better way? Turns out that unless you were willing to fork out some serous coin there wasn’t. Thus beginning our journey and Splits.


Together we are working to create a SAS application that can function efficiently and cheaply to not only resolve these issues for larger labels but the small indie artist as well. All without the headaches of a custom solution or that end up being more than your payouts.


As artists and managers ourselves we know firsthand the pains and challenges of growing sales in the industry and are working to resolve them for ourselves and you!

Planned features:

Song and album tracking including ownership percentages for artists and producers as well as publishing fees. Simple aggregation of sales data from multiple locations including Spotify Youtube Applemusic Tunecore and many others. Automatic tracking and calculations of payouts that need to occur for each individual with some ownership of a track. Automatic reports and for everyone showing where their earnings are coming from as well as more broad summaries for entries band or labels. Automatic payment processing for individuals. A solid solution to world hunger.

Sound Splits is a royalty accounting, software as a service, app built for indie music labels and songwriter collectives who need an easy to use, accurate and trustworthy royalty reporting system. Sound Splits helps you manage your most important asset-- your intellectual property-- by helping you keep your catalog organized and clean and most importantly understandable.
